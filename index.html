About Me

"Chemistry is not just a science — it's a lens through which I design solutions the world actually needs."

I'm a third-year BS-MS Chemistry student at IISER Bhopal — one of India's premier research institutions. But I don't just study chemistry. I build things with it.

From automating lab workflows to building medical simulation technology and AI-powered prosthetics, my work lives at the intersection of applied chemistry, electronics, and software.

I co-founded MedSimX to revolutionize medical training through realistic, feedback-driven simulators. I believe the best researchers are also builders — and the best founders are obsessive learners.

Key Projects

001
Automated Column Chromatography
Programmed and tested an automation system for column chromatography, reducing total system cost to $200. The setup includes dual grippers, automated fraction collector, and spot table — slashing human dependence and errors in compound purification. Benchmarked extensively against manual systems.
Automation ESP32 Chemistry Robotics
📄 Detailed PDF
002
AI-Based Voice Controlled Prosthetic Hand
Played a major role in developing GRASP — an AI-powered, voice-controlled prosthetic hand with servo attachments, N20 motor drives, pressure sensors across finger segments, and adaptive grip strength prediction. The system estimates the force required to hold objects of varying weight and fragility.
AI / ML Embedded Robotics 3D Printing
📄 Detailed PDF
003
Cyclic Voltammetry Soil Sensor
Contributed to IISERB's initiative for electrochemical soil testing using cyclic voltammetry — detecting soil nutrient profiles with precision and low cost, enabling smart agriculture applications.
Electrochemistry Sensors Agriculture
📄 Detailed PDF
004
Colour-Changing Sulphur Detector for Salt Extraction — Kutch, Gujarat
Developed a colour-changing chemical sensor designed to detect sulphur contamination during salt extraction in Kutch, Gujarat. The device provides real-time visual feedback — a colour change signal indicating sulphur presence — enabling field workers to make immediate quality decisions without expensive lab equipment. Includes a custom-designed extraction assembly and portable lab-scale prototype.
Chemistry Sensors SolidWorks Salt Industry Gujarat
