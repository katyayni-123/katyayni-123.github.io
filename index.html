About Me

“Precision of an engineer — it’curiosity of a chemist.”

I’m a Fifth-year BS-MS Chemistry student at IISER Bhopal — one of India’s premier research institutions. But I don’t just study chemistry. I build things with it.

From automating lab workflows to building medical simulation technology and AI-powered prosthetics, my work lives at the intersection of applied chemistry, electronics, and software.

Project Undertaken During Internship
Colour-Changing Sulphur Detector for Salt Extraction — Kutch, Gujarat
Developed a colour-changing chemical sensor designed to detect sulphur contamination during salt extraction in Kutch, Gujarat. The device provides real-time visual feedback — a colour change signal indicating sulphur presence — enabling field workers to make immediate on-site quality decisions without expensive laboratory equipment. Includes a custom-designed extraction assembly (SolidWorks CAD) and a portable lab-scale prototype.
