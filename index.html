About Me

"Chemistry is not just a science — it's a lens through which I design solutions the world actually needs."

I'm a third-year BS-MS Chemistry student at the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Bhopal — one of India's premier research institutions. But I don't just study chemistry. I build things with it.

From automating lab workflows to developing AI-powered prosthetic hands, my work lives at the intersection of applied chemistry, electronics, and software. I believe the best researchers are also builders — and the best founders are obsessive learners.

Currently, I'm exploring sensor design and electrochemical analysis, with a passion for making cutting-edge technology accessible and affordable.
